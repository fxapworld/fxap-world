FAQs
Where can I seek support and specific questions?
Visit the Wasabi Scripts official discord server here for support, updates, sales, and giveaways!
Does Wasabi Scripts support my specific framework?
Typically compatible out of the box with ESX, QBCore, and Qbox frameworks. There is also a system to adapt any framework!
What is the difference between 'escrow' and 'unlocked' versions?
An easy explanation of the difference between each version is simple.

Our 'escrow' versions have had a few files encrypted for the protection of the product but still extremely customizable.

Our 'unlocked' versions have no escrowed files, allowing for maximum customizations.
Can I upgrade my escrowed version to the unlocked version?
Yes, if we offer an unlocked version of the product. This is possible but will require you to open a ticket on our official Discord server.
How are my purchased assets delivered?
All Wasabi Scripts will be delivered via CFX's keymaster system. This ensures everyone can stay up to date.
Does Wasabi Scripts have a refund policy?
We do not offer any form of refund or exchange of any of our products. If you are unsatified or need assistance, reach out via Discord.
